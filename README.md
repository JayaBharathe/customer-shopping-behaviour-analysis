# Customer Shopping Behaviour Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes customer shopping behaviour to understand purchasing patterns, customer segments, and revenue drivers. An interactive Power BI dashboard was built to support business decision-making.

## Dashboard Preview
![Customer Behavior Dashboard](images/customer_behavior_dashboard.png)

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- Basic Data Modeling & DAX

## Key KPIs
- Number of Customers
- Average Purchase Amount
- Average Review Rating

## Analysis Performed
- Revenue and sales analysis by product category
- Customer segmentation by age group and subscription status
- Impact of shipping type on customer behaviour
- Gender-based purchasing insights

## Business Insights
- Clothing category contributes the highest revenue and sales
- Subscription customers form a smaller but valuable segment
- Young adults and middle-aged customers drive most sales
- Product category and age group strongly influence revenue patterns

## Deliverables
- Interactive Power BI dashboard
- Business-focused insights for decision-makers
